How it works

Many pepole from the industry call it a "Spy". However for most of the people - "Freezer" is a lot more useful.

It's a reversed mode of broadcast - it records everything that your stream contains. And by everything we don't mean only "every second". It's a lot more than that,

Freezer records all the metadata, events from mixing boards and whatever you need for creating reports that you consider useful: either for the purpose of techinal monitoring, and as well for the needs of royalties associations or government needs.

And it's a cloud solution with all its benefits - neither you don't have to be in your studio, to check what's going on at the moment; nor you have to be in your office, to work royalties reports. Be in a cafe and be as much effective.
